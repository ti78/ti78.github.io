Events


All Saints Day:

All Saints Day is a special event on November 1st, in which the All Saints Catholic Community gets together to celebrate and pray for all the Saints.


Curriculum Day:

Curriculum day is when All Saint's schools 4th Graders visited All Saints to get a taste of the high School's learning from years 7-10. This day is significant as it helps clear the students mind and give them an idea on which schools they should attend.
